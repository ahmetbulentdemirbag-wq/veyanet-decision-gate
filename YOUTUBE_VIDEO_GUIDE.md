# YouTube Video Guide

Target length: under 3 minutes.

Use the companion files:

- `VeyaNet_Decision_Gate_YouTube_Deck.pptx`
- `VeyaNet_Decision_Gate_YouTube_Narration.txt`

## Recommended Flow

1. Open with the problem.
   - Systems produce outputs, but not every output is safe to act on.
2. Introduce VeyaNet Decision Gate.
   - `1 = PASS`, `V = REVIEW`, `0 = BLOCK`.
3. Show the browser demo.
   - Switch between Hospital, Factory, and Project scenarios.
4. Explain the review route.
   - `V` keeps human review locked and creates evidence tasks.
5. Show the integrated pipeline schema.
   - Threshold calibration, negative control, audit ledger, revision loop, Q/V time-series, early warning.
6. State the safety boundary.
   - Decision support only; no autonomous medical, factory, or deployment authority.

## Closing Line

VeyaNet Decision Gate does not replace human judgment. It makes uncertainty visible, routes unresolved outputs to review, and records the decision path before action.
