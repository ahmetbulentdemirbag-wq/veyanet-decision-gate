Reference Architecture

The gate can sit after clinical systems, factory systems, DevOps pipelines, or agentic workflows. These references are context adapters; the protected VeyaNet core remains behind the final result interface.

Integrated Decision Pipeline Threshold calibration, negative controls, audit ledger, revision loop, and early warning route.
GitOps / Kubernetes Deployment outputs can be routed to 1 / 0 / V before release.
PostgreSQL Data Backbone Patient, audit, and operational records provide traceable evidence.
Agentic Loop Create, execute, diagnose, maintain, and document loops feed the gate.
Physical Operations Hospital, factory, and facility zones can be mapped into supervised actions.
